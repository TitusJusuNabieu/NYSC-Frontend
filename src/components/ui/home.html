About National Youth Service

The National Youth Service (NYS) is a government agency established by an Act of Parliament in 2016. Our mandate is to provide a structured platform for young graduates to participate in nation-building while developing their professional skills and competencies.

Through our one-year service program, we deploy graduates to various sectors where they contribute meaningfully to national development while gaining valuable work experience, leadership skills, and professional development opportunities.

What We Offer

Our Mission

To provide opportunities for young graduates to contribute to national development through structured service programs while gaining valuable work experience and professional skills.

Our Vision

To be the premier national service scheme that transforms young graduates into productive citizens and future leaders of Sierra Leone through structured service programs.

Core Values

We are guided by Professionalism, Integrity, Excellence, Innovation, and Patriotism in our service to the nation and development of young graduates.
